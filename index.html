About Me

About Me

My name is Annalisa Yin and I am a first year computer science student at the University of Waterloo. I am a Chinese - American who is currently living in Canada. I was born in Chicago, Illinois but don't really remember much of my life there. Being a big city definitely has its perks, ranging from The Bean to the beautiful skyline to the Chicago pizza, there's an endless amount of things to do. I highly recommend to visit Chicago whenever you guys get a chance!
Ottawa (the capital of Canada), where I currently live, is a smaller city full of harsh winters but warm people. The Rideau Canal (for annual skating trips), Beavertails, poutine, and the Parliament, I have no complaints about this wonderful city.
I always enjoy trying new things and expanding the number of hobbies I have. I love playing basketball, the guitar, and editing videos.
I am a Christian and have spent a lot of my time volunteering at my church leading bible studies and childrens summer camps.
