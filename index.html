About Me

About Me

My name is Annalisa Yin and I am a first year computer science student at the University of Waterloo. I am a Chinese - American currently living in Canada. While I was born in Chicago, Illinois I don't really remember much of my life there. Living in a big city definitely has its perks, ranging from tourist attractions to the beautiful skyline to the Chicago pizza, there's an endless amount of things to do. I highly recommend you to visit Chicago whenever there's a chance!
Now I currently live in Ottawa, the capital of Canada, it is a smaller city full of harsh winters but warm people. With the Rideau Canal for annual skating trips, classical Canadian food, and the Parliament, I have no complaints about this wonderful city.
I always enjoy trying new things and expanding the number of hobbies I have. I love playing basketball, editing videos, and learning guitar. Although I didn't have a lot of coding experience going into university, working hard and being a self learner has brought me to where I am today. I am currently exploring different side projects and am trying to learn new languages such as Python and frameworks like React.
I am a Christian and have spent a lot of my time volunteering at my church leading bible studies and childrens summer camps.
